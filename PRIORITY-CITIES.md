# Priority Search Cities — 15 Target Zones

## Strategy
Target high-tourism towns across astrocartography lines. Diversified tourism types (ski, wine, cultural, historic, mountain, river) — not exclusively ski resorts.

## COLORADO (5 cities)
1. **Telluride, CO** — Mountain tourism, ski, resort
2. **Breckenridge, CO** — Mountain tourism, ski, alpine
3. **Aspen, CO** — Mountain tourism, cultural, upscale resort
4. **North Fork Valley, CO** — Wine country, vineyard tourism, agricultural
5. **Westcliffe, CO** — Mountain tourism, outdoor recreation, scenic

## NORTHEAST + MID-ATLANTIC (10 cities)

7. **Cincinnati, OH** — Cultural tourism, riverfront, music/arts
8. **Shenandoah Valley, VA** — Wine country, scenic, historic tourism
9. **Finger Lakes, NY** — Wine country, lake tourism, culinary
10. **Vermont (Green Mountains)** — Foliage, mountain, cultural, scenic
11. **Asheville, NC** — Mountain tourism, arts/cultural hub, outdoor recreation
12. **Boone, NC** — High Country mountain tourism, outdoor recreation
13. **Chapel Hill/Durham, NC** — College town, cultural, historic tourism
14. **Georgetown, SC** — Historic riverside tourism, wine/restaurants (inland from Myrtle Beach)
15. **Pittsburgh, PA** — Cultural tourism, riverfront, historic neighborhoods
